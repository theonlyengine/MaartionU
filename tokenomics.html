Tokenomics

Understanding the economics behind MaartionBucks.

What are MaartionBucks?

MaartionBucks is our tokenized reward system that allows students to earn for their learning efforts and community participation. These tokens can be redeemed for various rewards or traded on our platform.

How it Works

Students earn MaartionBucks through academic achievements, active participation in classes, and contributing to the community. These tokens are recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and security.

Token Value

The value of MaartionBucks is driven by the demand within our platform. The more active the community, the more valuable the tokens become.
